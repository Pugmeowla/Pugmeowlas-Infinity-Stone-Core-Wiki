Pugmeowla’s Infinity Stone Core is the Official 1.20.1 port of Infinity Craft.

It brings the six Infinity Stones and the Infinity Gauntlet from the Marvel Cinematic Universe to life, complete with their unique powers, structures, and lore-based mechanics. Rather than being a simple recreation, the mod’s goal is to expand and modernize every aspect of the original Lucraft Core “Infinity experience” from 1.12.2, adapting it to the improved systems and flexibility of Palladium.

Infinity Stone Core blends seamlessly with popular mods such as Fsang’s Addonpack, Tinker’s Construct, and others, ensuring compatibility and creative freedom for modpack developers. Whether you’re collecting stones for power, exploring their mysterious worlds, or experimenting with cross-mod mechanics, this mod offers a fresh yet familiar experience for longtime Lucraft fans and newcomers alike.

From the shimmering Cosmi-Rod to the intricately designed stone structures scattered throughout the world, every feature has been tuned for immersion and fun, bringing a touch of the MCU’s epic scale into Minecraft.
