Obtainment

Go to Svartalfheim and find the Aether Chamber Underground. It contains the Aether inside. It will be guarded by Malekith. Shift + Right Click the Aether to remove the Reality Stone.

  • Shrink / Grow / Normal (Dummy): Allows for size changing; press to shrink, shift-press to grow, press again to return to original size.
