Obtainment

In the Overworld, find the Norse Village. It contains the Tesseract behind the tree. Shift + Right Click the Tesseract to remove the Space Stone.

Abilities

  • Teleport: Teleport to wherever you are looking.
  • Water Breathing: Allows you to breathe underwater.
  • Teleport Command: Unlocks the space_tp <x> <y> <z> command.
  • Telekinesis: Grab, move, and damage entities from a distance using telekinesis.
  • Force Field: Creates an impenetrable force field that makes you invulnerable but unable to move.
  • Fall Immunity: Negates all fall damage.
  • Step Assist: Allows you to step up full blocks without jumping.
  • Speed: Increases movement speed.
  • Black Hole (On): Creates a devastating black hole that consumes entities and blocks. Requires the Power Stone.
  • Black Hole (Off): Disables the active black hole.
