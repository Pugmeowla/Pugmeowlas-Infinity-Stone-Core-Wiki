Obtainment

In the Overworld, find the Sanctum Sanctorum. It contains the Eye of Agamotto inside a display case. Shift + Left Click the Eye of Agamotto to remove the Time Stone.

Abilities

  • Time Control (Scroll): Manipulate the flow of time by scrolling forward or backward. The sidebar shows time speed, with the center being normal.
  • Accelerate Time: Speeds up the passage of time, causing the world to move faster.
  • Reset Time Speed: Returns time to its natural state.
  • Time Freeze: Completely halts time. Press once to freeze, press again to resume.
  • Fast Forward: Rapidly advances the daylight cycle.
  • Stop Time: Stops the daylight cycle entirely until reactivated.
  • Start Time: Restarts the daylight cycle.
  • Projectile Freeze: Stops all projectiles midair. They regain momentum once you leave the area.
  • Time Set Command: Unlocks the time_set command, allowing direct control over world time.
