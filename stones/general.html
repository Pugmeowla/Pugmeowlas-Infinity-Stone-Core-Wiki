Obtainment

Find the structure associated with each stone and locate its container inside. Then Shift + Right Click the container to remove the stone.

Notes:

  • The Soul Stone does not have a container.
  • For the Time Stone (Eye of Agamotto), use Shift + Left Click to remove it, since it is an armor piece. Using Shift + Right Click will put it on the playerâ€™s chest slot.

Aspects of Existence

Inside Infinity Temples, you can find Shards of Existence. Craft them with Experience Bottles to create Aspects of Existence, which are used to upgrade and unlock abilities for the stones.

To make obtaining Experience Bottles easier:

  • Craft Fancy Bottles
  • Go to the Power Menu and buy the ability with experience points
  • Receive Experience Bottles as a result

Using the Stones

When all six stones are inserted into the Infinity Gauntlet, an animation will play. You can place the stones on your forehead, but only the Mind Stone will allow you to use its abilities.
