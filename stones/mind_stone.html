Obtainment

In The End, find the Sanctuary. It contains the Scepter on a floating chair. Shift + Right Click the Scepter to remove the Mind Stone.

Abilities

  • Energy Beams: The Scepter can shoot energy beams with the Mind Stone in it.
  • Flight: Grants you the power of flight. This ability can be toggled on or off if you wish to use another form of flight.
  • Intangibility: Allows you to phase through blocks and entities, becoming untouchable to the physical world.
  • Pacify: Pacifies hostile mobs around you, causing them to become peaceful. Sneak while activating to undo the effect.
  • Un-Pacify: Removes pacification from nearby hostile mobs, restoring their aggression.
  • Infinite Knowledge: Floods your mind with infinite knowledge, unlocking access to every crafting recipe.
  • Hero: Your presence inspires admiration and trust. Villagers see you as a true hero.
  • Night Vision: Enhances your perception, allowing you to see clearly even in complete darkness.
