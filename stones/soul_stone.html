Obtainment

Go to Vormir and find Vormir's Edge at coordinates 0,0. You will find the Soul Guardian waiting at the top of the mountain. To get the Soul Stone, you require a trade with the Best Friends Soul, which you obtain by killing your best friend—a tamed wolf—at the location by hitting it off the ledge. Trade with the Soul Guardian to get the Soul Stone.

Abilities

  • Glow: Shows all entities around you.
  • Pacify: Pacifies hostile mobs around you.
  • Immortality: Makes you completely immortal. Requires all stones to be inserted in the Gauntlet.
  • Infinite Saturation: Gives you infinite saturation.
  • Effect Immunity: Grants immunity to most negative status effects.
  • Invulnerability: Further enhances your immortality.
  • Summon Zombies: Summons seven zombies. Can be paired with the Mind Stone’s pacify ability to create a powerful army.
  • Infinite Health: Gives you nearly infinite health.
  • Enter Soul World: Allows you and nearby entities to enter the Soul World. Shift to exit.
  • Exit Soul World: Allows you and nearby entities to exit the Soul World.
  • Regeneration: Grants constant regeneration.
  • Locate Player Command: Unlocks the locateplayer <entity_name> command. Designed for players, but works with named entities.
